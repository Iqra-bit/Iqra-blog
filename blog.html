What is Iqra Pedia

Q: What is the Iqraraza.biz website about?

Iqraraza.biz is purely dedicated to English Literature Students who struggle through the process of making notes. It has a collection of English literature Notes, MCQs, writers’ biographies, summaries, and analyses of plays, novels, and poetry, literary terms, questions and answers, past solved papers.

Q: Who is the owner of the Iqraraza.biz?

It is a single-user website owned by Iqra Raza. ” Iqra Pedia” is the Pyseudonym of Iqra Raza.

Q: How Is this website different from others?

Ans: English Literature Notes provided on this website are completely owned by the owner of the website. Iqra Raza, the owner of the website is a Master’s in English Literature. She has a thorough knowledge of all the topics related to English Literature. The Language of the notes is intended to be simple and easy to understand yet the level of the Graduates and Masters’s level vocabulary has also been taken into account. The content on this website is not published and left however it is kept up to date according to the new criteria of the syllabus and the needs of the students.

Q: How old is this website?

Ans: This website was started in July 2021.

Q: What is the mission of Iqra Pedia?

Ans: Iqra Pedia is on a mission to empower students struggling with studies due to a lack of resources. Since education has gone digital the students prefer online resources to get the answers to their questions and solve assignments, so the English Literature Notes provided by Iqra Pedia fulfill the needs of any English Literature student. I remember the times when I used to be in a fix to manage between my job and studies. I am sure I am not the only person who struggled with this problem there are many still out there. Well, thanks to digital media where students have advanced to the digital learning process fortunately they have a chance to learn sitting at their pace.
